Tuition Fee

Details on the breakdown of school fee payments for all new applicants and returning students from 100 – 700 Levels

First Degrees and MB;BS Payment Structure for 2025/2026 Session

(a) 100 Students are required to pay 50 % of all due fees in Categories A and full payment for Categories B and C at the commencement of the session; 20% before the first-semester examination, 20% at the commencement of the second semester, and 10% before the second-semester examination.

(b) All Other Students (Levels 200 to 500) are required to pay 40% at the commencement of the session, 10% before the first-semester examination, 30% at the commencement of the second semester, and 20% before the second-semester examination.

Kindly note the following changes concerning school fee payment:

1. For 100 Level/Transfer/Direct Entry Students

70% payment of School fees is expected to be paid at the commencement of the session, while the balance of 30% is expected to be paid at the commencement of the second semester.

New Plan approved by Council:

  • 50% at the commencement of the session
  • 20% before the first-semester examination
  • 20% at the commencement of the second-semester
  • 10% before the second-semester examination

2. For 200-500 level students

50% payment of school fees is expected to be made at the commencement of the session, while the balance of 50% is expected to be made at the commencement of the second semester.

New Plan approved by Council:

  • 40% at the commencement of the session
  • 10% before the first-semester examination
  • 30% at the commencement of the second-semester
  • 20% before the second-semester examination

School Fees Payment for Foreign Students

Foreign Students living in Nigeria will pay a 10% increment in current school fees, while foreign students living outside Nigeria will pay a 15% increment in current school fees across all programmes.

MB;BS Clinical:

Three equal instalments payment is approved based on the timeline of the various clinical years. The length of the various levels differs.

Apply to Bowen

  • The school will not entertain any refund of fees after payment has been effected for whatever reason.
  • Overpayment of fees will be credited into students' account and sorted out at graduation if it has not been fully utilized by then.
  • All Students are required to register for their semester courses within the first three weeks of resumption. Registration is contingent on payment of all prescribed fees. Late registration after three weeks attracts a penalty of five per cent (5%) of whatever amount stands against the student as total outstanding fees.
  • No student shall be allowed to write the end of semester examination(s) until he/she has paid the prescribed fees.
  • All students are to pay their fees through any of the designated banks that render this service to the University.
