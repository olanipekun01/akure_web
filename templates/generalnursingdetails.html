General Nursing

The General Nursing programme at Achievers College of Nursing Sciences, Akure is designed to prepare students for a rewarding and impactful career in healthcare. The programme provides a strong foundation in nursing science, clinical practice, and professional ethics, equipping graduates with the skills and competence required to deliver safe, compassionate, and effective patient care. Students are trained through a blend of classroom instruction, laboratory practice, and supervised clinical experience across approved healthcare facilities.

Introduction to General Nursing

The General Nursing programme at ACONSA is structured to develop knowledgeable, skilled, and ethical nursing professionals.

Students gain a solid understanding of human anatomy and physiology, medical-surgical nursing, community health nursing, mental health nursing, maternal and child health, and professional nursing practice. Learn on your terms with pre-recorded engaging and interactive videos on your educational journey for flexible, convenient, and self-paced study.

Why you should apply:

The programme is taught by experienced nurse educators and healthcare professionals.

Strong emphasis on hands-on clinical training and practical competence.

Access to well-equipped nursing skills and simulation laboratories.

Supervised clinical postings in accredited hospitals and healthcare centres.

Graduates are well-prepared for the Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria (NMCN) licensure examination.

Training grounded in professionalism, ethics, and compassionate care.

Direct Entry Admission Requirements for General Nursing (RN)

Candidates seeking Direct Entry admission into the General Nursing programme must meet the following requirements as approved by the College and relevant regulatory bodies.

Direct Entry candidates must satisfy the required academic and professional qualifications:

  • Five (5) O’Level credit passes in English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics obtained in not more than two sittings.
  • Registered Midwives (RM) or holders of other relevant nursing-related professional qualifications recognized by the Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria (NMCN).
  • Candidates must possess a valid professional practicing license where applicable.
  • Evidence of completion of required professional training and certification from recognized institutions.
  • Successful performance at the College’s screening exercise and interview.
  • Candidates may be required to undergo medical fitness screening as part of the admission process.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the General Nursing programme are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and professional competence required to work in diverse healthcare settings. Our Registered Nurses are highly valued in both public and private health institutions.

Registered Nurse (RN)

Provide direct patient care, administer medications, monitor patient progress, and collaborate with healthcare teams in hospitals and clinics.

Community Health Nurse

Deliver preventive and promotive healthcare services within communities, focusing on health education and disease prevention.

Maternal & Child Health Nurse

Support mothers and children through antenatal, delivery, postnatal, and child healthcare services.

Mental Health / Psychiatric Nurse

Provide care and support for individuals with mental health conditions in specialized healthcare facilities.

Public Health Nurse

Work with government agencies, NGOs, and health organizations to promote population health and disease control.

Nursing Educator / Tutor

Teach and mentor future nurses in nursing schools and training institutions (with required qualifications).
