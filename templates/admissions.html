General Requirements for Applying to Study at Bowen:

  • Applicants must have sat for the 2025 Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME).
  • Applicants are required to have credits at WAEC, GCE, or NECO, in at least five (5) subjects, which must include English and four other subjects as may be specified by each College.
  • The five (5) credits should be obtained at not more than two sittings. (for Medicine and Surgery (MB; BS), Nursing Science and Physiotherapy programmes, the five credits must be obtained at only one sitting).
  • Candidates awaiting results may apply.
