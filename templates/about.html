Achievers College of Nursing Sciences, Akure (ACONSA)

A citadel of learning committed to raising competent, compassionate, and ethical nursing professionals equipped with knowledge, integrity, and character.

Who We Are

Achievers College of Nursing Sciences, Akure (ACONSA) is a reputable institution dedicated to the training and development of professional nurses who meet national and global healthcare standards. The College is committed to academic excellence, clinical competence, and character formation, preparing students to deliver quality, patient-centred care across diverse healthcare settings.

Rooted in strong ethical and Christian values, ACONSA provides a nurturing learning environment that promotes discipline, professionalism, and service to humanity.

Fully Accredited

Licensed by the National Universities Commission (NUC)

Our Commitment to Academic Excellence

At ACONSA, academic excellence is the foundation of our training. Our programmes are carefully designed to combine sound theoretical knowledge with intensive practical and clinical exposure. Through modern teaching methods, skilled faculty members, and well-equipped learning facilities, we ensure our students are fully prepared for professional nursing practice.

We emphasize:

* Quality teaching and learning

* Practical clinical competence

* Ethical and compassionate care

* Lifelong learning and leadership development

Our Journey

ACONSA was established to address the growing need for well-trained, ethical, and compassionate nurses in Nigeria and beyond. Since inception, the College has remained steadfast in its mission to produce graduates who are academically sound, clinically skilled, and morally upright.

Our Baptist Heritage

As an institution of the Nigerian Baptist Convention, Bowen University is firmly rooted in Christian values and principles. Our Baptist heritage informs our approach to education, emphasizing character development, service to humanity, and moral leadership.

The university's beautiful chapel stands as a symbol of our commitment to spiritual growth, providing a space for worship, reflection, and community building. Weekly chapel services bring together students, faculty, and staff in a shared expression of faith and fellowship.

While we maintain our Christian identity, we welcome students from all backgrounds, creating a diverse and inclusive community where mutual respect and understanding flourish.

The University

The University's Vision, Mission & Philosphy

Vision

To be the best university in Africa and indeed one of the best in the world; to produce a total person, morally sound, properly educated and entrepreneurially oriented, who would be useful to himself and to the society.

Mission

To provide the enabling environment – both physical and academic – for the production of competent and quality graduates who would be self-reliant, highly productive and globally relevant in every sphere of human endeavor.

Philosphy

The development of the total person in body, soul and character is to make him useful to himself and to the society at large. The concept of the total man with accentuated leadership potential, desire for self-employment and heightened community spirit is the underlying principle of Achievers University.
