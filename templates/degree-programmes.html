Achievers College of Nursing, Akure

COLLEGE OF NURSING SCIENCES

Achievers College of Nursing Sciences, Akure (ACONSA) provides high-quality nursing education designed to prepare students for professional practice in diverse healthcare settings. Our programmes emphasize academic excellence, clinical competence, ethical conduct, and compassionate patient care.

Welcome to the College of Nursing Sciences

Welcome to Achievers College of Nursing Sciences, Akure — a place where knowledge, skill, and character are developed for impactful nursing practice.

We are committed to delivering accessible, high-quality nursing education that equips students with the theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and professional values required to excel in today’s healthcare system. Our programmes are structured to ensure a strong foundation in nursing science alongside extensive clinical exposure.

Our faculty consists of highly qualified and experienced nurse educators and healthcare professionals who provide rigorous, engaging, and student-centered learning experiences. Through structured coursework, clinical postings, and mentorship, students are guided to achieve academic success and professional competence.

At ACONSA, we believe nursing is central to effective healthcare delivery and community well-being. As such, we focus on hands-on training, ethical practice, and lifelong learning. Students also benefit from clinical affiliations, supervised practical experiences, and professional development activities that prepare them for real-world healthcare challenges.
